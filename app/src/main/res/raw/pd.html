SHAKESPEARE BY THE SEA 2015 SEASON


Occurs July 1st, 2015 to September 6th, 2015


Shakespeare by the Sea is an outdoor theatre company operating out of Point Pleasant Park in Halifax Nova Scotia. Founded in 1994, SBTS performs original productions and Shakespearean classics at the beautiful Cambridge Battery in Point Pleasant Park. While the majority of the performances take place during the summer months, SBTS continues its season into the fall and winter, with shows taking place at the Bus Stop Theatre in Halifax. Entering its 22nd season, Shakespeare by the Sea strives to produce entertaining and affordable theatre for Halifax and its visitors.

THE 11TH ANNUAL HALIFAX SUMMER OPERA FESTIVAL


Occurs July 25th, 2015 to August 16th, 2015


Every summer for the past decade a large group of young singers (university undergrads, graduate students or young professionals) descend on Halifax to rehearse and perform in the Halifax Summer Opera Festival. Chosen by competitive auditions across Canada and the United States, this year we'll welcome over 80 singers on their journey towards public performances of their assigned roles. If you already love opera, this is a chance to hear the stars of tomorrow right here in Halifax, in challenging and thoughtful productions. If you're new to the art form, we've got a treat for you -- this is accessible, high energy music and theatre combined in a friendly, unstuffy way. Enjoy the infectious enthusiasm of our young casts as they sing and act their hearts out. guided by top professionals. Join us for our eleventh season as we present three fully-staged operas accompanied by piano, as well as extra concerts, master classes and special events. Our main performance venue is the James Dunn Theatre at Fountain School for the Arts, but we also perform at Neptune Studio Theatre and other venues around town.
